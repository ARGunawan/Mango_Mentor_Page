Learn from the best

Whether you are a rising senior, recent alumni, or someone who made a recent career change, we believe success is always achieveable.

Through mentorship, mentees will engage in the program to expand on their current skill set, knowledge, and educational background. By engaging with seasoned professionals mentees can gain insight into the secrets of success in their first role. Subjects our mentors provide advisory on are:

Technology Development

Mentees that want to explore a field in tech development will be paired with a mentor that is highly experienced in the following:

  • Software development
  • Data management
  • Block chain and Web3 development

Tech Leadership

Mentees interested in becoming a leader in the tech industry will be paired with a mentor that is highly experienced in the following:

  • Project Management
  • Technical Guidance
  • Team Management

Tech Sales and Marketing

Mentees interested in becoming a leader in the tech industry will be paired with a mentor that is highly experienced in the following:

  • Digital marketing
  • Sales fluency
  • Data analytics

A network of support and wisdom

Navigating the job industry is a daunting task for anyone trying to step into their first role and we strive to see everyone succeed!

Join an inclusive community of juniors, seniors and recent graduates who are looking to gain an advantage by learning from experienced professionals who have “been there and done that”, and are willing to share their life experiences.

Career Development

Mentees will have access to industry professionals with decades of experience in tech industry through:

  • Fostering a personal network
  • Expanding on foundational knowledge
  • Increasing confidence and communicaiton skills

Support

As you are taking your first step into the industry, we all need support and to feel advocated as the experience can be overwhelming. Support we provide are:

  • Advisory on career path
  • Address challenges mentees face
  • Provide feedback and focus on goals

Practical Application

Learn from successful professionals who already navigated through the industry. Our mentors help expand on foundational knowledge through:

  • One on one sessions
  • Learning activities
  • Mock interviews
